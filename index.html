The Bride and Groom

Sadhana and Jacky were supposed to have first met in primary school. Unfortunately, a few months prior to Jacky starting in what would have been the same year and class, Sadhana had left with her family to America.

The destiny of love could not be stopped however, as fate bought her back to Auckland to attend the same highschool a few years later. Destiny gave their love a second chance to blossom; it could not be stopped... except by akward teenage social behavior. Sadhana and Jacky went through 5 years of high school together, sharing classes and having mutual friends, but barely exchanging any words.

This seemed like a difficult case for Cupid to tackle, but the god of love did not give up. It waited until university to strike the couple with true love's arrow. It was during this often stressful and trying time as students that they were able to find and support each other, allowing a loving bond to bloom. Since then they have continued to support each other through good times and bad times. Now they are ready to take the next step and get hitched!
